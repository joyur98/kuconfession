What is this place

Have you ever wanted to say something — but stopped yourself because you knew people would judge you?

Have you ever carried something for weeks — a crush, a regret, a secret — with nobody to tell it to?

Have you ever been surrounded by people and felt completely, utterly alone with your thoughts?

Have you ever just needed to say it — to someone, to anyone — without having to explain yourself?

Have you ever wanted to scream something into the void and have strangers actually listen?

✦
This is why we exist

KU Confessions is a space built for anyone at Kathmandu University who has something to say but nowhere safe to say it. No account. No name. No face. Just your words, released into the void.

Maybe it's something small — a quiet crush you've never told anyone. Maybe it's something heavier — a feeling you've been carrying for a long time. Maybe you just need to vent about your day and have someone say "same."

Whatever it is, this is your room. Nobody here knows who you are. You speak. People listen. And they can reply — anonymously too.

How it works
✍️

Write it

No account needed. Pick a category, write freely, post anonymously.

💬

Get replies

Others can reply anonymously. Real conversations, no real names.

💛

Feel heard

Give hearts to ones that hit. Let people know they're not alone.

Community guidelines
✅

Always welcome

  • Real feelings, honest stories
  • Rants about life, stress, campus
  • Crushes, heartbreaks, love
  • LGBTQ+ experiences & stories
  • Kind, supportive replies
  • Humour and light-hearted posts
🚫

Not allowed

  • Nepali slurs — lado, puti, muji, randi, etc.
  • Naming & shaming real people
  • Bullying or targeting someone
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sexual content about real people
  • Threats or violent language

One thing to keep in mind

  • ✦ Anonymity is a gift — use it to be honest, not to be cruel. There are real people on the other side of this screen.
  • ✦ Offensive language, slurs, and posts targeting real people are automatically blocked — no exceptions.
  • ✦ When you reply to someone's confession, remember they were brave enough to share it. Respond with that in mind.
  • ✦ No personal data is collected. No account is needed. What you post here stays here.
